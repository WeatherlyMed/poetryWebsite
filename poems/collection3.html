Poem 1: Unnamed

Happiness is found in community. Success is found in community. Everything is comparative but it is also collectively made. From the one who wrote a textbook to the one who kept you sane Self made is a lie. Everyone else made you. You made them. So celebrate together. Cry together. One for all and all for one.
